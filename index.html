About Us

Small Giant Group is a fully Namibian-owned company that was established in 2023 and registered in July 2023 with the Ministry of Trade and Industry and other relevant business bodies.

We operate to various specifications and employ dedicated and qualified staff. The group provides services to different clients depending on their needs.

Our team is equipped with Industry 4.0 Employability Skills such as Analytical Thinking and Innovation; Reasoning, Problem-solving and Ideation; Complex Problem-solving; Critical Thinking and Analysis; Creativity, Originality and Initiative; Leadership and Social Influence; Technology use, Persuasion and Negotiation.
